About The Patrician News :

Owner Name : Samuel raj

Patrician News of India, established in 2001, is a TamilNadu minority Honest and Informational self-financing News Firm affiliated to the Government Of India.

It was started by the head of Minority people , a congregation established by jackson on 2nd February 1993, in India,Tamilnadu. The members of the congregation carry inspiration from the life and spirit of their jackson - Patrician. With a noble spirit to serve humanity, the Patrician people stepped into the vocation of educating the youth to free them from the bonds of ignorance. With their exceptional skill in leadership, governance and the strong hand of the Almighty, the congregation has established NEWS Firms , schools & colleges throughout the world.

In consonance with the philosophy of people congregation, the patrician firms aims at providing quality education and true NEWS to the Citizens of today with utmost sincerity. To facilitate this, the management has been keen in building the faculty with qualified Reporters and teachers, pooling in resources from different platforms, and installing state-of- the art infrastructural facilities. Moreover, the News firm backs up students with a number of support services on and off campus. Along with curricular and co-curricular activities, the the Patrician News Firm constantly insists that a sense of social conscience has to be instilled in the minds of citizens. In view of this, several outreach programmes are conducted wherein citizens enthusiastically participate and do a better service.
